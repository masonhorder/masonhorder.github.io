Step 1: Physical Design(3D Printing)

  1. The first part is to gather your files and which languages you are going to chose. Download from here to check out full list of options. Download each of the options: Frame, Top, Side 1, Side 2, Side 3, Side 4, Side 5 and Base
  2. For printing the sides you need to use a technique of printing the logo with one file and the back in another. Line each print up or use the merge tool to to print seamlessly in two colors with only one extruder. Refer to this video for further instructions.
  3. For printing the frame and base you simply slice and print in black, supports are needed for the frame
  4. Once printing is complete remove all supports and keep handy for next part

Step 2: Physical Design(Hardware)

  1. The first thing is too collect all of the necessary procuts
    • ESP32
    • Lithium Ion Battery
    • Battery Charger
    • Gyro/Accelerometer
    • Angle USB
  2. Take the side piece that is labeled Cube Coding Log and snap it into place, this will now be reffered to as the top.
  3. Now take the ESP32 and solder it to the Gyro. Solder the wires to the top of the pin headers so that the pins are free to slide into there place on Side 1. Screw the Gyro in with 2 M3*8. Solder 2 wires to recive power from the charger. Snap Side 2 in front of the top.
  4. Take Side 2 and place it to the right of the top, no electronics held on it
  5. Take Side 3 and slide the Litium Ion 18650 cell into place. Snap Side 3 behind the top
  6. Take Side 4 and place it to the right of the top, no electronics held on it
